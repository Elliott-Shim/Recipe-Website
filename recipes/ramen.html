Ramen

Description

Ramen is a noodle dish of Japanese origin that is famous for its umami-rich broth and its perfectly cooked noodles. The recipe described here is for a shoyu ramen.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 3 cups stock
  • 3 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 packages of chukamen noodles
  • green onions
  • nori
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat 1 teaspoon sesame oil in a deep pan over medium heat and saute 1 clove garlic until fragrant.
  2. Lower the heat and add 3 cups stock into the pan. Bring to a boil.
  3. Add 3 tablespoon soy sauce, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon sugar to soup.
  4. In a separate large pot, bring water to a boil. Add chukamen noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Use a fine-mesh strainer over a bowl to seperate the soup from the solids.
  6. Drain the noodles and add to soup.
  7. Top with green onions, nori, and black pepper to taste.
