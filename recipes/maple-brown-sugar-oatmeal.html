Maple and Brown Sugar Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a common and beloved staple in many American homes, consisting of simple ingredients and easy to prepare, its simple yet delicious taste is unmistakable.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3/4 cup oats
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup

Steps

  1. Boil water in small pot, add oats and stir for one minute.
  2. Remove from heat and add brown sugar and maple syrup. Continue stirring for 2-3 minutes.
